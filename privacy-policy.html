Privacy Policy — upload-bot

Effective Date: May 30, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how upload-bot ("the Application", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use upload-bot, a content scheduling and publishing tool for creators.

1. About upload-bot

upload-bot is a content scheduling application that allows creators to schedule and automatically publish short-form videos to social media platforms including TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook. upload-bot is an independent tool and is not affiliated with TikTok, YouTube, or Meta (Facebook).

2. Information We Collect

upload-bot collects only the minimum information required to operate:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authorize upload-bot via TikTok Login, we receive your TikTok user ID and display name. We store access tokens on your local device to publish videos on your behalf.
  • Video Content: Video files you choose to schedule for upload via upload-bot.
  • Post Metadata: Titles, descriptions, hashtags, and scheduled publish times you provide to upload-bot.
  • Upload Logs: Local logs of upload activity stored on your device.

3. How upload-bot Uses Your Information

upload-bot uses your information solely to:

  • Authenticate with the TikTok Content Posting API using the video.publish permission you grant
  • Upload and publish videos to your TikTok account at scheduled times through upload-bot
  • Display upload history within upload-bot for your reference

upload-bot does not sell, share, or transfer your personal data or TikTok content to any third party. upload-bot does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or analytics.

4. TikTok API and Permissions

upload-bot integrates with the TikTok Content Posting API. By connecting your TikTok account to upload-bot, you authorize upload-bot to use the following TikTok permission:

  • video.publish — to post videos directly to your TikTok account

upload-bot requests only the video.publish scope and no additional TikTok permissions. Your TikTok access token is stored securely on your local device (macOS Keychain) and is never transmitted to upload-bot's servers or any third party.

TikTok's Privacy Policy governs data processed by TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Data Storage and Security

upload-bot stores all data locally on your device:

  • TikTok access tokens are stored in the macOS Keychain (AES-encrypted)
  • Upload queue and logs are stored in local files on your computer
  • upload-bot does not operate any remote servers or cloud storage

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You may delete upload-bot's data at any time by removing the application and its associated files from your device. To revoke TikTok access granted to upload-bot, go to your TikTok account settings → Apps and Websites → Manage Connected Apps and remove upload-bot.

7. Children's Privacy

upload-bot is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of upload-bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how upload-bot handles your data, please contact us at: over100storiesaday@gmail.com
